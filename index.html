Fighting for Freedom ... to the death, because it's your life they took

Gratitude for the everyday, Paul Robinson shares joys and hardships since his release

Written by Joli Holmes and Jovi Dai

This story documents Paul Robinson's life after his release from prison, where he served 52 years. Paul maintains his innocence and is working with the New England Innocence Project (NEIP) to overturn his conviction in his fight for justice. He was 75 years old when he was released and celebrated his first birthday in over 50 years with family this past December. We spoke to Paul in January 2023, approximately eight months after release.
